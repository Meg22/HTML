South Indian Breakfast

There is nothing better than good Indian food!

Indian food has a variety of different ingredients, but tends to rely on some, more than others. Indian food can be relatively healthy, because it uses the following products:



Ingredient Property
Turmeric Antiseptic
Cumin Aids Digestion
Ginger Helps soothe throatache
Chilli Increases circulation
Coriander Ease Migraines
Garlic Makes everything better!



There is nothing quite like an idly or a dosa to start your day!

Most south indian dishes contain mustard seeds, and dried red chillis. The curries contain a variety of different lentils such as dried split pigeon peas, and mung dal. The chutneys are made from freshly grated coconut, and usually have a sprinkling of lemon juice and coriander leaves.

This is what a south indian breakfast looks like. Dosas and Idlies!
